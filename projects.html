Behavioral Public Economics — Research Map
D3.js · Force Graph YAML Ontology Bernheim & Taubinsky (2018) · NBER Boot Camp

An Obsidian-style knowledge graph of behavioral public economics research. Maps the full ontology of the Bernheim–Taubinsky (2018) handbook chapter and NBER Behavioral Public Economics Boot Camp reading list — covering corrective taxation, nudges & defaults, present bias, social insurance, household finance, and behavioral IO.

Korea Policy Evaluation — Research Map
D3.js · Force Graph YAML Ontology Top Journals: AER · QJE · JPE · REStud · Econometrica

An interactive knowledge graph of top-journal papers for Korean policy evaluation. Organizes papers by policy area — fertility, education, housing, labor markets, social insurance, and regional policy — and tags each paper as a Korea-specific study 🇰🇷, a comparable international template 🌐, or a core methodology paper 📐. Designed to answer: "Which papers should I reference for this Korean policy question?"
